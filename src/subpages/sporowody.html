Sporo Wody

Sporo Wody is a music project I started in 2020. My compositions have mainly focused on ambient, inspired by early synthesisers, tape music, and new age aesthetics. The new material goes in a slightly different direction, focusing on minimalism, textures and harmonies. It has been described as a combination of Twin Peaks' atmosphere and Minecraft music. Drawing inspiration and utilising elements of dreams, I leverage them to study matter, its properties, characteristics and attributes.

So far I've released two cassetes - Aloha Park (under Enjoy Life Label) and Orange Is a Snack (under BAS label)

Third cassete will be released in 2025
