About

I engage with topics of nostalgia, childhood sentiments, and the realms of hypnagogia. They emerge as recurring leitmotifs, rooted in the depths of subconscious feelings, shaping the essence of my creative expression. Utilising elements of my dreams, I leverage them to study matter, its properties, characteristics, and attributes. Operating within video, music, and new media, I aim to create a narrative that offers a fresh perspective on established paradigms

Moje prace były pokazywane m.in.: na Chroma Art Festival Miami (US), Copenhagen Light Festival (DK); Extinction Rebelion w Studio Teatrgaleria (PL), oraz Kulturnatten 2023 (DK). Performatywnie udzielam się w zespole Chair oraz jako solowy, elektroniczny projekt Sporo Wody.
